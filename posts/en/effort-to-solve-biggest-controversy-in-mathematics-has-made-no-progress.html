Efforts to Resolve Mathematics' Greatest Controversy Remain Stalled

www.newscientist.com · Tue, 21 Jul 2026 14:13:38 +0000

For over a decade, mathematicians have failed to reach a consensus on whether the ABC conjecture has been proven, with recent efforts to resolve the controversy making no significant progress.

For more than a decade, the international mathematical community has remained locked in an unprecedented deadlock over the claimed solution to the ABC conjecture, one of number theory's most profound and important open problems. The controversy originally erupted when a prominent mathematician published an extraordinarily lengthy and complex series of papers purporting to offer a complete proof. However, because the work introduced an entirely novel, highly abstract theoretical framework, scholars worldwide have struggled to thoroughly digest, verify, or reach a unanimous agreement on its overall validity.

Recent and renewed efforts to break this long-standing impasse and definitively settle the debate have continued to struggle without making any tangible progress. Over the past several years, numerous specialized workshops, international academic conferences, and focused peer-review initiatives have been organized to carefully examine the hundreds of pages of dense mathematical reasoning. Despite these extensive endeavors, prominent critics maintain that fundamental flaws remain unaddressed, while proponents steadfastly defend the correctness of the proof, leaving the field deeply divided.

The persistent failure to reach a consensus underscores the significant challenges modern mathematics faces when attempting to evaluate exceptionally complex theoretical breakthroughs. Without a universally accepted verification or a simplified explanation that satisfies key experts, the status of the ABC conjecture remains suspended in an unusual state of scientific ambiguity. As fresh attempts to bridge the gap between opposing factions continue to falter, mathematics' biggest contemporary controversy shows no clear sign of being resolved anytime soon.
