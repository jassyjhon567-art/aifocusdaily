New Study Reveals AI Visibility in ChatGPT Depends on Topic-Level Strategy

www.semrush.com · Mon, 20 Jul 2026 13:04:00 +0000

A study of 50,000 brands shows that visibility in ChatGPT is driven by topic-level strategy, offering new opportunities as most categories currently lack dominant leaders.

A comprehensive new study analyzing 50,000 brands within ChatGPT interactions has revealed that brand visibility in generative artificial intelligence platforms is fundamentally a topic-level game. As digital discovery rapidly evolves and millions of users rely on AI assistants for recommendations, understanding how these models select and surface information has become vital for business visibility. The findings demonstrate that success in conversational search depends on establishing authority within specific sub-topics rather than relying solely on traditional overarching brand recognition or high-level domain authority.

According to the research, a vast majority of topic sectors inside ChatGPT currently have no single dominant brand commanding recommendations. Unlike traditional search engine results where legacy industry leaders frequently secure top positions across broad query categories, conversational AI outputs display significantly higher fragmentation and fluidity. This dynamic landscape presents an unprecedented window of opportunity for rising enterprises, smaller entities, and specialized content creators to secure prominent mindshare by targeting specific thematic verticals before these digital spaces become fully saturated.

Navigating this fundamental shift requires a strategic realignment of digital marketing practices toward generative engine optimization and contextual content distribution. Brands hoping to maximize their AI presence must focus on delivering deep, authoritative value and building clear entity associations that large language models can easily parse, cross-reference, and trust. As conversational interfaces increasingly replace standard search channels for consumer research and decision-making, mastering topic-level optimization will likely differentiate the future digital market leaders from those left unseen in the rapidly expanding AI ecosystem.
