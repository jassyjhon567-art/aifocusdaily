Expert Designer Tips to Instantly Elevate Your Bathroom Remodel

www.goodhousekeeping.com · Sat, 18 Jul 2026 18:00:00 +0000

Professional interior designers share simple yet impactful remodeling ideas, ranging from vibrant wallpapers to elegant countertop upgrades, to instantly elevate any bathroom space.

Refurbishing a bathroom is widely considered one of the most effective ways to increase both the aesthetic appeal and the overall market value of a home. While a complete overhaul can often feel daunting and expensive, leading interior design experts suggest that strategic, targeted updates can yield dramatic results. By focusing on key visual and functional elements, homeowners can easily transform an outdated, utilitarian bathroom into a luxurious, spa-like sanctuary without undergoing a full-scale renovation process.

Among the most highly recommended upgrades is the introduction of statement wallpaper. Designers frequently advocate for bold patterns, botanical prints, or textured wallcoverings in powder rooms and guest baths to create an immediate, captivating focal point. Additionally, replacing worn or dated countertops with high-quality materials like quartz, granite, or marble can instantly elevate the room's sophistication. Updating smaller details, such as replacing old faucets, cabinet hardware, and light fixtures with modern finishes like brushed brass or matte black, also provides a cohesive and polished look.

To complete the aesthetic transformation, experts emphasize the critical importance of lighting and mirrors. Replacing a standard, builder-grade mirror with a custom-framed or uniquely shaped alternative can completely redefine the scale of the entire space. When paired with layered lighting solutions—such as adding elegant wall sconces alongside soft overhead fixtures—the bathroom immediately becomes warmer and more inviting. Ultimately, these designer-approved adjustments prove that thoughtful, incremental changes can make a substantial impact, easily turning any bathroom into a beautifully curated retreat.
