Sam Altman and Jony Ive Partner to Shape the Future of AI Hardware

fortune.com · Wed, 22 Jul 2026 07:01:00 +0000

Sam Altman and former Apple designer Jony Ive have partnered to create innovative AI hardware, facing legal and competitive challenges as OpenAI expands beyond software.

OpenAI Chief Executive Officer Sam Altman and legendary former Apple chief designer Jony Ive have joined forces in an ambitious effort to revolutionize hardware for the artificial intelligence era. By combining OpenAI's cutting-edge generative models with Ive’s world-renowned design philosophy, the high-profile dream team aims to create an entirely new class of personal technology products. This strategic collaboration reflects OpenAI's broader initiative to build a hardware ecosystem beyond software, securing a distinct competitive edge over major tech rivals in the rapidly expanding AI landscape.

However, this ambitious new venture faces significant strategic headwinds, including escalating friction and potential legal disputes with Apple. As OpenAI ventures into manufacturing physical consumer devices, it strays directly into competitive territory long dominated by established global tech giants. Tapping Apple’s former hardware design mastermind has heightened corporate scrutiny regarding intellectual property rights and former non-compete agreements. Navigating these complex legal and operational hurdles will be absolutely critical as the artificial intelligence startup seeks to fundamentally transform how ordinary users interact with technology on a daily basis.

Despite these formidable commercial and legal challenges, the vision of an AI-first hardware product remains central to OpenAI’s long-term business strategy. The initiative highlights a growing technology industry consensus that traditional screen-centric smartphones may no longer serve as the ideal interface for next-generation conversational AI systems. As Sam Altman and Jony Ive continue to quietly refine their secret joint project, the ultimate outcome could permanently reshape the consumer electronics market and determine whether OpenAI can sustain its pioneering global leadership.
